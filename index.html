About HealBites

HealBites is an AI-powered nutrition platform designed for individuals managing chronic conditions like diabetes, hypertension, and kidney disease. By combining the latest clinical guidelines with personalized recommendations, HealBites empowers users to take control of their health one meal at a time.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between medical care and everyday nutrition, offering patients and healthcare providers a smarter, easier way to improve outcomes.

The Need

Empower Patients

Tailored meal plans to promote better health outcomes. HealBites personalizes nutrition not only to health needs, but also to cultural preferences, helping patients embrace sustainable, long-term change.

Support Providers

With limited time for nutritional counseling, doctors need efficient tools. HealBites delivers ready-to-use plans based on clinical guidelines, saving time and improving care.

Bridge the Gap

Chronic diseases drive 90% of U.S. healthcare spending, costing over $4.1 trillion annually. HealBites aims to reduce preventable hospitalizations and healthcare expenditures by empowering nutrition-driven disease management.
